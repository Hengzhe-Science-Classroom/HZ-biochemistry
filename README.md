# Biochemistry · Interactive

**Proteins, enzymes, metabolism, and molecular biology**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 5 · Research Frontier

## What You'll Learn

- Amino acids and protein structure
- Enzyme kinetics and mechanisms
- Carbohydrates and glycobiology
- Lipids and membranes
- Metabolism: glycolysis, TCA, oxidative phosphorylation
- DNA replication, transcription, and translation
- Signal transduction

## References

- David L. Nelson & Michael M. Cox, *Lehninger Principles of Biochemistry* (8th ed., W.H. Freeman)
- Jeremy M. Berg, John L. Tymoczko & Lubert Stryer, *Biochemistry* (9th ed., W.H. Freeman)
- Donald Voet & Judith G. Voet, *Biochemistry* (4th ed., Wiley)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos

- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
